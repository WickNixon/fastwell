# PRODUCT_VISION.md — Fastwell

## The Founder's Why

Women in midlife are not lacking motivation. They are lacking consistency — and a system that supports them when no one is watching.

One-on-one coaching is out of reach for most members financially. The generic health apps on the market were built for 25-year-olds. Every diet and wellness trend promises a quick fix that fades within weeks. And women in perimenopause and post-menopause are navigating one of the most complex hormonal shifts of their lives with almost no tools built specifically for them.

Fastwell is the answer to all of that. It is a coach in their pocket — built around the same fasting and lifestyle philosophy taught inside the membership, available 24 hours a day, never judging, always celebrating. It gives women the structure and accountability they need to build habits that actually last.

For the business, Fastwell is also a member retention tool. When a member can see how far they have come — her HbA1c improving, her sleep lengthening, her energy climbing over three months — she does not leave. Fastwell makes the results visible.

---

## The Problem We're Solving

Most health apps were not built for this woman:
- Generic trackers focus on calories, streaks, and step counts
- They do not account for hormonal cycling, HRT, brain fog, or menopause-specific biomarkers
- They reward daily fasting streaks — which is actively harmful for cycling women
- They frame weight loss as the primary goal, which is both reductive and demotivating
- They offer no coaching layer — just data with no context
- They surface no meaningful patterns — just numbers without insight

Women in this community are motivated and investing in their health. What they are missing is a single, warm, intelligent place that holds all their information together — reflects it back in a way that helps them understand their body — and makes them feel proud of every step forward.

---

## The Vision

Fastwell is the health companion built exclusively for the menopausal woman.

It brings together fasting, lifestyle habits, biomarkers, supplements, AI-powered pattern insights, and wearable integrations into one beautiful, easy-to-use app — personalised from the very first question it asks. It helps women in their 40s, 50s, 60s, 70s, and 80s feel more in control of their health, have better conversations with their doctors, and understand what is actually working in their body.

- Tech comfort: Low to moderate
- Goals: Track HbA1c improvements, manage weight, get GP-ready data
- Relationship to app: 14-day free trial, then $18.99/month or $159.52/year

- Age: 45–65, follows Dr. Mindy Pelz protocols closely

## Business Model

### Tier 1 — Member Access (Wicked Wellbeing community)
- Access via **personal invite link** generated from the admin dashboard
- Single-use link, pre-validates email, expires after 7 days
- **3 months completely free**, then **50% off forever**
- Monthly after trial: **$9.50 NZD/month**
- Annual after trial: **$79.76 NZD/year** (~$6.65/month)
- 7 days before 3-month trial ends → personalised Resend email, member tone

### Tier 2 — Subscriber Access (general public)
- Standard signup with email + password
- **14-day free trial**, full feature access, no credit card at signup
- Day 15 → full-screen paywall, must actively choose a plan to continue
- Monthly: **$18.99 NZD/month**
- Annual: **$159.52 NZD/year** (30% saving — $13.29/month equivalent)
- 3 days before trial ends → Resend email focused on annual plan value
- In-app cross-promo message: "Wicked Wellbeing members get 50% off Fastwell — plus weekly coaching calls and a community of women just like you."

### Pricing Rationale
At $18.99/month, Fastwell is positioned as a specialist, premium tool. The demographic is underserved, motivated, and willing to invest in their health. The annual plan's 30% discount drives longer commitment and reduces churn. The member discount of 50% creates a meaningful and visible incentive to join the Wicked Wellbeing membership — the app becomes a recruitment tool for the community.

### Member Retention Strategy
Fastwell increases membership retention by making results visible over time. A member who can see her HbA1c dropping, her sleep improving, and her energy rising over six months does not cancel her membership. The app is the evidence of the membership's value.
